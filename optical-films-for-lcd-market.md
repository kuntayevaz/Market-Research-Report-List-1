<p><ul><li>Reflective Films</li><li>Brightness Enhancement Films</li><li>Optical Diffusion Films</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical films for LCD market includes three types: reflective films, brightness enhancement films, and optical diffusion films. Reflective films are designed to increase the amount of light reflected from the LCD screen, thereby improving image visibility under ambient light conditions. Brightness enhancement films enhance the overall brightness and color saturation of the LCD display, enhancing the viewing experience. Optical diffusion films scatter light, reducing the visibility of ambient light reflections on the screen and improving image quality. These films are crucial components in LCD technology, enhancing performance and user experience.</p></p>

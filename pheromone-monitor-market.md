<p><p>The pheromone monitor market consists of various types of traps used for capturing insects. The bottle trap is a cylindrical container with a small entrance that allows insects to enter but prevents their escape. The funnel trap has a wide top and a narrow neck, allowing insects to easily enter but making it difficult for them to exit. The delta trap is shaped like a triangle and captures insects when they fly into its adhesive surface. Other types of traps used in the market include sticky traps and bucket traps. These traps help in monitoring and controlling insect populations.</p></p>

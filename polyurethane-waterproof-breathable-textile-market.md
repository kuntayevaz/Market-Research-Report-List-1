<p><p>The polyurethane waterproof breathable textile market is divided into three types: densely woven, coated, and membrane market. Densely woven textiles are made by tightly weaving the fabric, which creates a barrier against water while allowing air to pass through. Coated textiles have a layer of polyurethane coating applied to the fabric, which makes it waterproof and breathable. Membrane textiles consist of a thin polyurethane membrane that is sandwiched between layers of fabric, offering superior water resistance and breathability. These different types cater to various industries and consumer needs.</p></p>

<p><p>The PVC floor market comprises various types, including homogenous, heterogeneous, vinyl tiles (VT), and luxury vinyl tiles (LVT). </p><p>Homogenous PVC floors are made of a single layer with consistent composition, providing durability and resistance to wear and tear. Heterogeneous PVC floors have multiple layers, offering a wide range of design options. Vinyl tiles (VT) are individual tiles made of PVC, allowing for easy installation and replacement. Luxury vinyl tiles (LVT) replicate the look of natural materials like wood or stone, providing a high-end aesthetic with the added benefits of durability and affordability.</p></p>

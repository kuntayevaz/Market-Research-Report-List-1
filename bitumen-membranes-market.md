<p><strong>The Bitumen Membranes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>APP</li><li>SBS</li><li>SEBS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bitumen membranes market is categorized into three major types, including APP, SBS, and SEBS. APP (Atactic Polypropylene) is a thermoplastic polymer added to bitumen to increase its temperature resistance. SBS (Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene) is a synthetic rubber that enhances the flexibility and durability of bitumen. SEBS (Styrene-Ethylene-Butadiene-Styrene) is a type of elastomer that provides better weathering resistance and elongation properties to bitumen. These types of membranes are widely used in various construction projects such as roofing and waterproofing due to their excellent performance attributes.</p></p>

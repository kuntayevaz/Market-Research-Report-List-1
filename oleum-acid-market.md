H4S2O8, also known as pyrosulfuric acid, is a strong oxidizing agent and finds application in manufacturing dyes and drugs. Lastly, H6S3O12, called tripyrosulfuric acid, is a highly reactive compound used in the synthesis of organic chemicals and as a desiccant. All three types play crucial roles in multiple industrial processes.</p></p>

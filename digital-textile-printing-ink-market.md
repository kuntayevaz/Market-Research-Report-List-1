<p><p>Digital textile printing ink is available in various types, each catering to different printing needs. Reactive dye inks are commonly used for natural fibers like cotton and produce vibrant, long-lasting colors. Acidic inks are suitable for synthetic fibers and provide excellent color fastness. Paint inks offer a wider color gamut and are ideal for printing on a variety of textiles. Dispersion inks are used for high-resolution printing on polyester fabrics. Sublimation inks are specially formulated for heat transfer and produce vivid, durable images on polyester fabrics.</p></p>

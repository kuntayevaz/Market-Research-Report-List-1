<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Aluminium Rolling Oil market comprises two main types: Cold Rolling and Hot Rolling. Cold Rolling involves passing the aluminium sheets through multiple rollers to achieve the desired thickness and surface finish. It is commonly used for manufacturing products like cans, foil, and automotive parts. Hot Rolling, on the other hand, involves heating the aluminium ingots and then passing them through rolling mills. This process is employed for producing structural components, plates, and other applications that require higher strength. Both types play a crucial role in the aluminium industry, catering to different product requirements.</p></p>

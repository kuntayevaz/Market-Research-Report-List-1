<p><p>Spray paint comes in two main types: solvent-based and water-based. Solvent-based spray paint is made with organic solvents like acetone or xylene and provides a durable and long-lasting finish. It is commonly used for outdoor applications and on materials such as metal or plastic. On the other hand, water-based spray paint is made with water as the main solvent and offers a lower level of toxicity. It is suitable for indoor applications and is often used on materials such as wood or drywall.</p></p>

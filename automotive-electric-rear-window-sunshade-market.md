<p><p>The automotive electric rear window sunshade market consists of three types: scissors, C-pillar guide, and others. Scissors sunshades are typically made of interlocking metal panels that unfold to cover the rear window. C-pillar guide sunshades make use of vertical guides attached to the vehicle's C-pillar to control the movement of the shade. Other types include shades that are controlled by electronic motors or operated manually using buttons or switches. All these types serve the purpose of providing shade and privacy to the vehicle's rear window.</p></p>

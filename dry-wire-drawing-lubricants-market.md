<p><p>Dry wire drawing lubricants are used in the wire drawing process to reduce friction and heat, extending the life of the dies and improving the quality of the wire. Sodium soap-based lubricants are commonly used in dry wire drawing applications due to their excellent lubricating properties. They provide good lubrication and a smooth surface finish. Calcium soap-based lubricants offer similar benefits but are preferred for higher-stress wire drawing operations. Other types of dry wire drawing lubricants include borate-based, phosphate-based, and chloride-based lubricants, each with their unique properties and applications. These lubricants play a critical role in enhancing the efficiency and performance of the wire drawing process.</p></p>

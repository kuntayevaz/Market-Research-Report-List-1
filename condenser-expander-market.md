<p><p>A condenser expander is a mechanical device used in power plants for converting the low-pressure steam exhausted from the turbine condenser into mechanical energy. This mechanical energy is then used to drive various auxiliary equipment such as pumps, compressors, and fans. Condenser expanders help improve the overall efficiency of power plants by utilizing the energy that would otherwise be wasted.</p><p>The condenser expander market is witnessing significant growth due to the rising demand for power, particularly from developing countries. The increasing focus on renewable energy sources, such as solar and wind, is also driving the market as condenser expanders play a crucial role in these power generation systems. By type, the market can be classified into single-stage and multi-stage condenser expanders.

<p><p>The catalysts used in the polypropylene market can be categorized into three main types - Ziegler-Natta catalyst, metallocene catalyst, and others. Ziegler-Natta catalysts are traditional catalysts that allow the production of polypropylene with a wide range of properties and high crystallinity. Metallocene catalysts are newer and offer enhanced control over molecular structure, resulting in improved strength and transparency of polypropylene. The "others" category includes various catalysts with unique characteristics, such as single-site catalysts, which enable the production of polypropylene with exceptional overall performance.</p></p>

<p><p>High-performance plastics refer to a group of materials that possess exceptional mechanical, thermal, chemical, and electrical properties. The market for high-performance plastics can be categorized into various types, including fluoropolymers (FPS), high-performance polyamides (HPPA), polyphenylene sulfide (PPS), sulfone polymers, liquid crystal polymers, and others. Fluoropolymers offer excellent chemical resistance, high thermal stability, and low friction properties. High-performance polyamides exhibit high strength, stiffness, and heat resistance. Polyphenylene sulfide is known for its excellent electrical properties and dimensional stability. Sulfone polymers possess high transparency, mechanical strength, and resistance to high temperatures. Liquid crystal polymers provide exceptional strength, stiffness, and heat resistance.</p></p>

<p><p>The MBS resin market encompasses two main types: transparent MBS and non-transparent MBS. Transparent MBS resins are characterized by their ability to transmit light and provide clarity, making them suitable for applications like clear packaging films and optical lenses. On the other hand, non-transparent MBS resins lack transparency and are commonly used in applications that require impact resistance and toughness, such as automotive components and construction materials. Both types serve distinct purposes based on their physical properties and are widely utilized in different industries.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Architectural Insulating Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Insulating Glass Units</li><li>Low-E Insulating Glass Units</li><li>Traditional Reflective Insulating Glass Units</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Architectural Insulating Glass market comprises three main types of glass units. Conventional Insulating Glass Units consist of two or more glass panes separated by a spacer filled with air or gas, providing thermal insulation benefits. Low-E Insulating Glass Units have a thin coating that reduces the transfer of heat, improving energy efficiency. Traditional Reflective Insulating Glass Units have a pyrolytic coating that reflects heat from the sun while allowing ample natural light, enhancing both energy savings and comfort.</p></p>

<p><p>The Textile Antistatic Agent market can be categorized into four types based on their chemical properties: anion, cation, zwitterion, and nonionic. Anion antistatic agents contain negatively charged ions, while cation agents have positively charged ions. Zwitterion agents possess both positive and negative charges in their molecules, allowing them to balance the charges on textile surfaces. Nonionic agents do not carry any charge. These different types of antistatic agents offer varying degrees of static electricity prevention in textiles, enabling manufacturers to choose the most suitable option for specific applications.</p></p>

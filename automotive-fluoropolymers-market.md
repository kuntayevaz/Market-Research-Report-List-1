<p><p>The automotive fluoropolymers market comprises various types of materials used in the automotive industry. Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) is a popular type known for its high temperature resistance and excellent chemical resistance. Ethylene tetrafluoroethylene (ETFE) is another type used for its exceptional electrical properties and durability. Fluorinated ethylene propylene (FEP) offers good non-stick characteristics and low friction. Fluoroelastomer (FKM) provides excellent resistance against heat, chemicals, and fuel. The "Others" category includes other types of fluoropolymers that are utilized in specific automotive applications. These materials play a critical role in ensuring performance, reliability, and safety in automotive components.</p></p>

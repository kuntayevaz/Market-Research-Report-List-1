<p><p>The in-vehicle camera lens market can be categorized into two types: imaging and inductive. Imaging cameras use various technologies like CCD or CMOS to capture images and videos. They are widely used for surveillance, parking assistance, and driver assistance systems. On the other hand, inductive cameras use electromagnetic induction principles to capture images. They are commonly utilized for machine vision applications, particularly in harsh environments where dust, dirt, or chemicals may affect image quality. Both types offer distinct features and benefits, catering to different requirements in the automotive industry.</p></p>

<p><p>Lignin products are derived from lignin, a complex organic polymer found in plant cell walls. The market for lignin products can be divided into three primary types: Kraft Lignin, Lignosulphonates, and Others. Kraft Lignin is obtained during the pulping process and is used as a raw material in various industries. Lignosulphonates are produced through the sulfite pulping process and find applications in construction, animal feed, and agriculture. The "Others" category includes lignin-based materials used in fuel production, carbon fibers, and composites. These lignin products contribute to sustainable and eco-friendly alternatives in various sectors.</p></p>

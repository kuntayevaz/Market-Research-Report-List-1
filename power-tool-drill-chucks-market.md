<p><p>The Power Tool Drill Chucks Market comprises three main types of drill chucks: key-type drill chucks, keyless drill chucks, and self-tightening drill chucks. Key-type drill chucks require a special key to tighten or loosen them, providing precise control and stability. In contrast, keyless drill chucks can be operated by hand, allowing quick and convenient bit changes. Self-tightening drill chucks have a mechanism that automatically tightens the chuck as the drill is activated, ensuring a secure grip on the bit without the need for manual adjustment.</p></p>

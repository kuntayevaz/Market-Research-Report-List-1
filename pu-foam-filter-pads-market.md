<p><p>PU foam filter pads are commonly used in industrial and household applications to filter air and liquids. There are two main types of PU foam filter pads: polyester foam filter pads and polyether foam filter pads. Polyester foam filter pads are known for their high durability and resistance to chemicals, making them suitable for harsh environments. On the other hand, polyether foam filter pads are known for their excellent air and moisture permeability, making them ideal for applications requiring good airflow and water drainage. Both types of filter pads offer effective filtration and are widely used in various industries.</p></p>

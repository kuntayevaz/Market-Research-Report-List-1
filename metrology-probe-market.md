<p><p>The metrology probe market comprises two major types: machine tool probes and coordinate measuring machine (CMM) probes. Machine tool probes are used in the manufacturing industry to measure and inspect workpieces during machining operations. They enable automated in-process measurements, ensuring accuracy and minimizing human error. On the other hand, CMM probes are used in quality control applications to assess the dimensional accuracy of finished parts. They provide precise measurements of complex geometries and are essential for maintaining quality standards in various industries. Both types of probes contribute to streamlining manufacturing processes and enhancing product quality.</p></p>

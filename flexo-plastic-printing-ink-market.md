<p><p>Flexo plastic printing ink is a type of ink used in flexographic printing, specifically for printing on plastic materials. There are three main types of flexo plastic printing ink markets: </p><p>1. Water-Based Inks: These inks are environmentally friendly and consist of a water base mixed with colorants and resins. They have low VOC emissions, are easy to clean, and provide good print quality.</p><p>2. Solvent-Based Inks: These inks contain solvents that evaporate during the printing process, leaving behind the colorants and resins. They offer excellent adhesion, fast drying time, and are suitable for a wide range of substrates.</p><p>3. UV-Cured Inks: These inks are dried and cured instantly through exposure to ultraviolet light. They offer high print quality, fast production speeds, and excellent adhesion to various plastic substrates.</p></p>

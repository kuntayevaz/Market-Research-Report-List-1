<p><p>Asphalt emulsifiers are used in road construction and maintenance to make asphalt bind well with water. Ionic asphalt emulsifiers have a positive or negative charge, helping to stabilize the asphalt-water mixture. They are commonly used in high-traffic areas with heavy loads. Non-ionic asphalt emulsifiers lack an electrical charge and are more versatile. They are used in various climates and alongside different aggregates. Both types of emulsifiers play a crucial role in ensuring durable and long-lasting asphalt pavements suitable for different environmental conditions and traffic loads.</p></p>

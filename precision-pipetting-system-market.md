<p><ul><li>Multi-Channel</li><li>Single-Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Precision Pipetting System market consists of two types: Multi-Channel and Single-Channel. Multi-Channel systems enable simultaneous pipetting of multiple samples, increasing efficiency and reducing time. These systems are well-suited for high-throughput applications and laboratories processing large volumes of samples. On the other hand, Single-Channel systems are designed to pipette one sample at a time with high precision and accuracy. They are commonly used in applications requiring lower sample volumes or when flexibility and precision are essential. Overall, both types cater to different needs within the laboratory environment.</p></p>

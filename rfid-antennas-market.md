<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Active RFID antennas are equipped with their own power source, allowing them to transmit signals and receive data from RFID tags. They offer a longer range and higher reading speed compared to passive RFID antennas, making them suitable for tracking objects in real-time. On the other hand, passive RFID antennas rely on the power generated by the RFID reader to transmit signals and gather data. They are cost-effective and have a shorter range, making them ideal for applications like inventory management and access control systems.</p></p>

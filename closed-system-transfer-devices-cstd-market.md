There are primarily two market types for CSTDs - developing closed systems and air-cleaning systems. Developing closed systems aim to prevent drug contamination during transfer, minimizing the risk of exposure. On the other hand, air-cleaning systems focus on removing any aerosolized or vaporized drugs from the surrounding air to enhance safety. Both types of CSTDs play a crucial role in minimizing the potential health hazards associated with handling hazardous drugs.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Brake Chamber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piston Type Brake Chamber</li><li>Diaphragm Type Brake Chamber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brake chamber market consists of two main types: piston type brake chambers and diaphragm type brake chambers. </p><p>Piston type brake chambers use a piston mechanism to apply force to the brake shoes, causing them to engage with the brake drum and slow down the vehicle. These chambers are commonly used in heavy-duty vehicles due to their robustness and high braking power.</p><p>On the other hand, diaphragm type brake chambers rely on a flexible diaphragm to generate the force needed for braking. These chambers are typically found in light to medium-duty vehicles, as they are less complex and more cost-effective.</p><p>Both types play a crucial role in ensuring safe braking performance in different vehicle applications.</p></p>

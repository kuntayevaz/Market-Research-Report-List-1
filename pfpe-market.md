<p><p>PFPE (perfluoropolyether) refers to a type of synthetic lubricant that is widely used in various industries. There are two main market types: PFPE oil and PFPE grease. PFPE oil is a thin liquid lubricant that provides long-lasting protection against friction and wear. It is commonly used in high-speed machinery and equipment. PFPE grease, on the other hand, is a thick lubricating substance that is more suitable for applications that require enhanced resistance to extreme temperatures, harsh chemicals, and high pressures. It is commonly used in industrial, automotive, and aerospace applications.</p></p>

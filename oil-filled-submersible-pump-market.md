The vertical type submersible pump is installed vertically in a well or borehole. The horizontal type submersible pump is placed horizontally at the bottom of a reservoir or tank. The inclined type submersible pump is positioned at an angle, allowing water to be lifted from a lower level to a higher level. These pump types serve different purposes and are designed to meet specific requirements in various industrial applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The multi-mode microplate reader market is categorized into three types: absorbance, fluorescence, and luminescence. Absorbance is used to measure the amount of light absorbed by a substance, providing information about the concentration of molecules in a sample. Fluorescence measures the emission of light from a sample after excitation with a specific wavelength, enabling the detection of specific molecules or reactions. Luminescence detects the emission of light from a sample generated by chemical reactions, which helps study cellular processes or ATP-based assays. These technologies are widely used in research, drug discovery, and diagnostics.</p></p>

Carbon Crystal heaters use a thin layer of heating element made from carbon crystal materials to generate heat efficiently and evenly, resulting in quick and direct heating. On the other hand, Carbon Fiber heaters utilize conductive carbon fiber elements to generate heat, providing a more gentle and radiant warmth. Both types are energy efficient, cost-effective, and offer a modern and sleek design, making them popular choices in the market.</p></p>

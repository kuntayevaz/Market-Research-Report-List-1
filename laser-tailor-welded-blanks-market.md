<p><p>Laser tailor-welded blanks (LTWBs) are a type of material used in the manufacturing process of automobiles, specifically in the body-in-white (BIW) structure. With the increasing demand for lightweight and fuel-efficient vehicles, LTWBs offer a cost-effective solution for automakers. This further drives the growth of the laser tailor-welded blanks market.</p><p>Moreover, technological advancements in laser welding and automation are expected to further boost market growth.

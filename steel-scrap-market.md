<p><p>The steel scrap market is divided into three main types: flat steel scrap, long steel scrap, and tubular steel scrap. Flat steel scrap refers to scraps from sheets or plates, such as metal cuttings or leftover materials from manufacturing processes. Long steel scrap includes scraps from products like beams, bars, or rods. Tubular steel scrap consists of scraps from pipes or tubes. Each type has its own market with varying demands, pricing, and recycling processes.</p></p>

<p><p>The water-soluble cored solder wire market consists of two types: halogen-free and halogen-contained. Halogen-free solder wire is manufactured without the use of halogens such as chlorine or fluorine. This makes it an environmentally friendly option as it does not release harmful gases when soldering or during disposal.

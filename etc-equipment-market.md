<p><strong>The ETC Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RSU</li><li>OBU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ETC (Electronic Toll Collection) equipment market encompasses various types of devices used for efficient toll collection on highways and bridges. RSU (Roadside Unit) is one such device installed on the roadside that communicates with vehicles passing by to collect tolls electronically. On the other hand, OBU (On-Board Unit) refers to the device installed in vehicles that facilitate communication with the RSU to deduct toll amounts automatically. Both RSU and OBU play crucial roles in the ETC system, ensuring seamless and convenient toll collection, thereby reducing traffic congestion and improving overall transportation efficiency.</p></p>

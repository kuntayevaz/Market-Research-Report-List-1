<p><strong>The Radiation Shielding Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traditional Lead Shielding Materials</li><li>Lead Composite Shielding Materials</li><li>Lead-Free Shielding Materials</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiation shielding materials market comprises three main types: traditional lead shielding materials, lead composite shielding materials, and lead-free shielding materials. Traditional lead shielding materials, commonly used in the past, are heavy but highly efficient in blocking radiation. Lead composite shielding materials are a combination of lead and other elements, offering increased strength and flexibility. Lead-free shielding materials are a relatively newer alternative, made from materials like tungsten or bismuth that provide effective radiation protection without the environmental concerns associated with lead.</p></p>

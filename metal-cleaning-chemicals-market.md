<p><p>The metal cleaning chemicals market can be classified into two types - water-based metal cleaning chemicals and solvent metal cleaning chemicals. Water-based metal cleaning chemicals are formulated with water as the main ingredient, making them environmentally friendly and safer to use. They are effective in removing dirt, oil, and grease from metal surfaces. On the other hand, solvent metal cleaning chemicals are formulated with organic solvents like alcohol, acetone, or mineral spirits. They are highly effective in removing stubborn contaminants from metal surfaces but are often more hazardous to handle and carry a higher environmental impact.</p></p>

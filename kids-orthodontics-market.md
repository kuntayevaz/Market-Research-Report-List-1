<p><p>The kids orthodontics market includes various types of dental treatments. Aligners are clear plastic trays that gradually move teeth to their desired position. Retainers are used after braces or aligners to prevent teeth from shifting. Dental wires are thin metal wires used in conjunction with braces to apply pressure and move teeth into place. These treatments aim to improve the dental alignment and overall oral health of children.</p></p>

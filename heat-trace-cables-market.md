<p><strong>The Heat Trace Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-Regulating / Self-Limiting</li><li>Constant-Wattage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the heat trace cables market, there are two main types: self-regulating/self-limiting and constant-wattage. Self-regulating/self-limiting heat trace cables have the ability to adjust their power output according to the surrounding temperature. This makes them more energy-efficient and helps prevent overheating. On the other hand, constant-wattage heat trace cables have a fixed power output, providing a consistent level of heat regardless of the temperature. They are commonly used in applications where a constant and specific heat output is required. Both types have their own advantages and are suited for different heating needs.</p></p>

<p><p>The Gigabit Passive Optical Network (GPON) Equipment market consists of three main types - Optical Line Terminal (OLT), Optical Network Terminal (ONT), and Passive Optical Splitters. The Optical Line Terminal (OLT) acts as the central hub, connecting the service provider's network to the customer's premises. The Optical Network Terminal (ONT) is installed at the customer's premises and serves as the interface between the user's devices and the OLT. Passive Optical Splitters are used to split the optical signal into multiple channels, allowing multiple users to share the same fiber optic cable.</p></p>

<p><p>Active noise-cancelling (ANC) headphones are a type of headphones that use advanced technology to reduce or eliminate external sounds by producing anti-noise signals. These signals are then combined with the original soundwave, resulting in a quieter and more immersive listening experience. ANC headphones are particularly beneficial in noisy environments such as airplanes, busy city streets, or crowded offices, as they can significantly reduce the background noise and allow users to focus on their audio content.</p><p>In recent years, the active noise-cancelling headphones market has witnessed significant growth. One of the key factors driving this growth is the increasing demand for premium audio products, coupled with the growing popularity of portable music devices. The rise in disposable income and changing consumer preferences towards wireless headphones have also contributed to the market's expansion. Additionally, advancements in technology, such as the integration of AI and machine learning algorithms, have improved the performance of ANC headphones, further boosting their demand.</p><p>The market is also driven by the increasing adoption of ANC headphones in industries such as aviation, healthcare, and call centers, where noise cancellation plays a crucial role in enhancing communication and reducing stress.

<p><strong>The Hydrophobic Silica Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fumed</li><li>Precipitated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydrophobic silica refers to silica particles that display a strong aversion to water. Fumed silica is a type of hydrophobic silica produced through high-temperature hydrolysis of silicon tetrachloride. It finds applications in various industries including paints, coatings, and adhesives due to its exceptional properties like high surface area and reinforcement capabilities. Precipitated silica is another hydrophobic silica variant prepared by neutralizing sodium silicate with sulfuric acid. It is widely used as a reinforcing filler, anti-caking agent, and thixotropic agent in rubber, plastics, and food industries. Both fumed and precipitated silicas contribute significantly to the hydrophobic silica market.</p></p>

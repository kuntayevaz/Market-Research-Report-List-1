<p><p>Drop On Demand (DOD) inkjet inks are used in various applications, such as printing, coding, and marking. The market for DOD inkjet inks is classified into two types: thermal DOD and piezoelectric DOD. In thermal DOD, tiny droplets of ink are ejected as a result of a heat pulse generated by a resistor. It is widely used in consumer and industrial printing applications. On the other hand, in piezoelectric DOD, droplets are ejected by the deformation of a piezoelectric material, offering precise control and versatility, thus finding applications in high-resolution printing and complex graphics.</p></p>

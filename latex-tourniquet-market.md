<p><p>The latex tourniquet market comprises two types: manual and pneumatic. Manual tourniquets are commonly used in medical procedures and are operated by hand to control blood flow. They are ideal for small-scale applications and provide accurate pressure control. On the other hand, pneumatic tourniquets utilize inflatable cuffs that are connected to an automatic pressure control system. These tourniquets are more suitable for larger procedures, as they offer consistent pressure regulation and reduce the risk of human error. Both types play a crucial role in medical settings, ensuring safety and efficiency during various procedures.</p></p>

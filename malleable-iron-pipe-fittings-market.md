<p><p>The malleable iron pipe fittings market consists of various types of fittings. Coupling fittings are used to connect two pipes together. Elbow fittings are utilized to change the direction of the pipe system. Tee fittings are designed to create a T-shaped connection between three pipes. Additionally, there are other types of fittings available in the market, including bushings, caps, plugs, and unions, which serve different purposes such as reducing or closing off pipe connections. These fittings are essential components in plumbing and piping systems across various industries.</p></p>

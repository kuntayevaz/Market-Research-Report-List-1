Cyclosporin, Tacrolimus, Everolimus, Sirolimus, and Mycophenolic Acid are different types of immunosuppressive agents commonly used in the market. Cyclosporin and Tacrolimus work by inhibiting the action of certain immune cells, while Everolimus and Sirolimus target a protein involved in cell proliferation. Mycophenolic Acid inhibits the production of DNA required for immune cells. These drugs play a crucial role in managing various immunological conditions.</p></p>

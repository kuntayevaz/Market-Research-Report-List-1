<p><ul><li>Water-based Label Adhesive</li><li>Solvent-based Label Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Label Adhesive Market comprises two major types: Water-based Label Adhesive and Solvent-based Label Adhesive. Water-based Label Adhesive is a type of adhesive that is made by dissolving adhesive polymers in water. It is widely used due to its low toxicity, low volatile organic compound emissions, and environmental friendliness. Solvent-based Label Adhesive, on the other hand, is made by dissolving adhesive polymers in organic solvents. It offers excellent adhesion properties, fast drying time, and high resistance to water and heat.</p></p>

<p><p>Mine-resistant Ambush Protected (MRAP) vehicles are designed to protect military personnel from improvised explosive devices (IEDs) and ambushes in combat zones. There are two types of MRAPs: MRAP-MRUV and MRAP-JERRV. MRAP-MRUV stands for Mine-Resistant Utility Vehicle, which are smaller, highly mobile MRAPs used for transportation and resupply missions. MRAP-JERRV stands for Joint EOD Rapid Response Vehicle, which are larger, heavily armored vehicles used for explosive ordnance disposal (EOD) and other specialized missions. Both types provide enhanced protection and survivability for military personnel in high-threat environments.</p></p>

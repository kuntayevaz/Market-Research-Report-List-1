<p><p>The Very Low Frequency (VLF) Antennas market is divided into two types: Receiving Antennas and Transmitting Antennas. Receiving Antennas are designed to capture VLF signals from the atmosphere and convert them into electrical signals for transmission to electronic devices. Transmitting Antennas, on the other hand, send out VLF signals into the atmosphere, which are then picked up and decoded by receiving antennas. These antennas play a crucial role in communication and navigation systems, particularly in military applications where long-range and reliable communication is essential.</p></p>

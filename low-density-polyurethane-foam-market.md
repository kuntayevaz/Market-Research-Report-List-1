<p><p>Low-Density Polyurethane Foam (PUR) is available in two market types: Soft Low-Density PUR and Rigid Low-Density PUR. Soft Low-Density PUR is a flexible foam with a low density, making it ideal for applications such as cushioning, mattresses, and upholstery. It offers excellent comfort, conformability, and durability. On the other hand, Rigid Low-Density PUR is a foam with a low density and high strength. It is commonly used in insulation, packaging, and structural applications due to its thermal insulation properties and structural stability. Both types of foam cater to different industry needs and provide unique benefits.</p></p>

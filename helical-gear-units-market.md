<p><p>Helical gear units are a type of mechanical device used for power transmission and speed reduction in machinery. The market for helical gear units can be divided into two types: parallel-axis and perpendicular-axis. </p><p>Parallel-axis helical gear units are designed for applications where the input and output shafts are parallel to each other. They offer high efficiency and can handle high torque loads, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications.</p><p>Perpendicular-axis helical gear units are used when the input and output shafts are at a 90-degree angle to each other. They are commonly used in applications where space is limited and can provide smooth and quiet operation.</p><p>Both types of helical gear units offer reliable and efficient power transmission, but their design and application considerations differ based on the shaft orientation.</p></p>

<p><p>The road traffic coating market includes various types of coatings used for road markings. Paint is one such type, which is applied directly on the road surface and provides good visibility. Thermoplastic coatings, on the other hand, are heat-applied and offer durability and skid resistance. Preformed polymer tape is a pre-cut tape with adhesive backing, which is easy to apply and provides long-lasting markings. Epoxy coatings are highly resistant to wear and tear and are typically used in high-traffic areas. These coatings cater to different needs and requirements in the road traffic industry.</p></p>

<p><p>COVID-19 testing kits are medical devices used to detect the presence of the SARS-CoV-2 virus, which causes the COVID-19 disease. There are two types of testing kits available: molecular tests (such as PCR tests) that detect the virus's genetic material, and antigen tests that identify specific proteins on the virus's surface.</p><p>The current outlook of the COVID-19 testing kits market is highly positive. The market witnessed significant growth in 2020 due to the global pandemic. The demand for testing kits surged as governments and healthcare organizations worldwide ramped up testing efforts to control the spread of the virus. The market was dominated by molecular testing kits, given their higher accuracy. However, as the pandemic progressed, antigen testing kits gained popularity due to their faster results and affordability.</p><p>The future of the COVID-19 testing kits market remains optimistic as the detection of new variants and the possibility of future outbreaks are expected to drive ongoing demand.

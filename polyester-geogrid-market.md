<p><p>Polyester geogrid market types include biaxial tension and uniaxial tension. Biaxial tension geogrids have equal strength in both the longitudinal and transverse directions, providing excellent reinforcement and stabilization properties for soil or asphalt structures. On the other hand, uniaxial tension geogrids mainly possess high strength in one direction, allowing them to efficiently distribute load and control the movement of soil or aggregate materials. These market types cater to different project requirements and offer diverse solutions for enhancing the stability and durability of various construction applications.</p></p>

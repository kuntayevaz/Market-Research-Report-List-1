Tiled bathrooms, as the name suggests, consist of ceramic or stone tiles on the walls and floors. FRP complete bathrooms are constructed using fiberglass-reinforced plastic, providing high resistance to moisture and corrosion. Color steel plate complete bathrooms are made of color-coated steel plates, ensuring strength and longevity. Each type offers different advantages and is suitable for various preferences and requirements.</p></p>

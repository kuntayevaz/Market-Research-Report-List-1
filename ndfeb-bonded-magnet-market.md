<p><p>NdFeB bonded magnets are widely used in various industries due to their high magnetic properties. Injection molding is a type of manufacturing process in which molten NdFeB magnetic powders are injected into a mold to form the desired shape. This method is suitable for complex shapes and high production volume. On the other hand, pressing molding involves pressing NdFeB magnetic powders into a desired shape using pressure and heat. This method is suitable for producing magnets with higher magnetic performance and lower dimensional tolerance requirements. Both methods offer efficient and cost-effective solutions in the production of NdFeB bonded magnets.</p></p>

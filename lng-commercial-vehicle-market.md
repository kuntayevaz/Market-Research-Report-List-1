<p><p>The LNG (liquefied natural gas) commercial vehicle market comprises two main types: Spark Ignition and Hybrid Compression Ignition. Spark Ignition vehicles use a spark plug to ignite the natural gas and have a simpler design, making them more cost-effective and suitable for smaller vehicles like vans and pickup trucks. On the other hand, Hybrid Compression Ignition vehicles combine both spark ignition and compression ignition technology, providing increased power and efficiency. They are commonly used for larger commercial vehicles such as buses and trucks.</p></p>

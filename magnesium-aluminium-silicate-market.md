MAS is widely used in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, ceramics, and paints, due to its excellent characteristics.</p><p>In terms of market analysis, the global Magnesium Aluminium Silicate Market is expected to witness significant growth in the forecast period. The increasing consumer awareness regarding personal grooming and appearance is expected to fuel the demand for cosmetic products, thereby positively impacting the market growth.</p><p>Another factor contributing to the market growth is the growing pharmaceutical industry.

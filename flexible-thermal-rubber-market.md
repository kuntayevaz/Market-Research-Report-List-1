<p><strong>The Flexible Thermal Rubber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NBR Based</li><li>EPDM Based</li><li>Chloroprene Based</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flexible thermal rubber market consists of various types such as NBR (Nitrile Butadiene Rubber) based, EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) based, Chloroprene based, and others. NBR-based rubber is known for its excellent oil resistance and is commonly used in automotive and industrial applications. EPDM-based rubber offers good weather resistance and finds applications in roofing and automotive seals. Chloroprene-based rubber exhibits flame resistance and is used in electrical insulation and cables. The "Others" category includes rubber types like silicone and fluoroelastomers, which are used in specialized applications such as aerospace and medical industries.</p></p>

<p><p>The hot method iron and steel slag market consists of two types: blast furnace slag and steelmaking slag. Blast furnace slag is a byproduct of the iron-making process and is generated during the cooling and solidification of molten slag. It is commonly used in construction materials and cement production. On the other hand, steelmaking slag is produced during the steel-making process and is characterized by its high calcium and iron content. It finds applications in road construction, soil improvement, and as a raw material in cement production.</p></p>

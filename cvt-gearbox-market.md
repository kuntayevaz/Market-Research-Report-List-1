<p><strong>The CVT Gearbox Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Belt-Chain Drive CVT</li><li>Planetary CVT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CVT gearbox market can be categorized into two types: Belt-Chain Drive CVT and Planetary CVT. In Belt-Chain Drive CVT, a belt or chain is used to transmit power between the pulleys, offering a simple and cost-effective solution. On the other hand, in a Planetary CVT, a system of gears is employed to generate various gear ratios seamlessly. This type of CVT is known for its efficiency and smooth operation. Both Belt-Chain Drive CVT and Planetary CVT serve the purpose of continuously adjusting gear ratios to optimize the engine's performance and fuel efficiency.</p></p>

<p><p>The low salt soy sauce market consists of two main types: brewed and blended. Brewed soy sauce is made by fermenting soybeans and wheat together with salt and a specific mold called "koji." This traditional method takes months and results in a rich and complex flavor. On the other hand, blended soy sauce is made by mixing brewed soy sauce with other ingredients like water, vinegar, or additives. Blended soy sauce is quicker to produce and often has a milder taste compared to brewed soy sauce.</p></p>

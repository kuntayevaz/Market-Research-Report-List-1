<p><p>The adhesives for medical wearables market can be categorized into three types: acrylics based, silicone based, and others. Acrylics based adhesives are widely used as they offer strong bonding and high resistance to moisture. Silicone based adhesives are known for their skin-friendly properties, biocompatibility, and flexibility. They are widely used in medical applications due to their gentle adhesion and ease of removal. The "others" category includes adhesives made from various materials like polyurethane and hydrogels, which offer specific properties like breathability and enhanced skin adhesion.</p></p>

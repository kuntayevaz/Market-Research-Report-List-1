<p><p>Solid Phase Extraction (SPE) columns are used in various industries to separate and purify samples in laboratories. Non-polar columns are utilized for non-polar compounds such as hydrocarbons and drugs. Lastly, other types of SPE columns include mixed-mode columns which have a combination of both polar and non-polar functionalities to extract a wide range of compounds.

<p><p>A battery electric bus, also known as an electric bus or e-bus, is a type of bus that is powered by electricity stored in a battery pack. Battery electric buses are typically equipped with regenerative braking, which helps extend their range by converting kinetic energy into electric energy.</p><p>The future of the battery electric bus market looks promising, with a strong growth trajectory. The rising awareness about environmental concerns, government initiatives to reduce carbon emissions, and the increasing focus on sustainable transportation are driving the demand for battery electric buses.

<p><p>The automotive emissions ceramics market includes three major types: honeycomb, GPF (Gasoline Particulate Filter), and DPF (Diesel Particulate Filter). Honeycomb ceramics are used in catalytic converters to convert harmful gases into less harmful ones. GPF is specifically designed for gasoline vehicles to reduce particulate matter emissions. It filters out tiny particles from the exhaust gases. DPF, on the other hand, is used in diesel vehicles to trap and eliminate soot and other particulate matter. These ceramics play a crucial role in reducing vehicle emissions and improving air quality.</p></p>

<p><p>Peristaltic pumps are commonly used in mining and mineral extraction applications. The market for these pumps includes two main types: roller peristaltic pumps and shoe type peristaltic pumps. Roller peristaltic pumps utilize rotating rollers to compress and move fluid through the pump, while shoe type peristaltic pumps use a flexible shoe to create a squeezing action. Both types are effective in handling abrasive and corrosive fluids, making them ideal for the demanding conditions encountered in mining and mineral extraction processes.</p></p>

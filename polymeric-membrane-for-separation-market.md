<p><ul><li>Hollow Fiber</li><li>Spiral Wound</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polymeric membrane for separation market is classified into three types: hollow fiber, spiral wound, and others. Hollow fiber membranes consist of long, tubular fibers that allow for efficient separation due to their large surface area. Spiral wound membranes are made up of thin polymer sheets rolled around a permeate collection tube, making them ideal for high-pressure applications. The "others" category includes various membrane configurations such as tubular, plate and frame, and capillary. Each type of membrane offers unique advantages and is utilized based on specific separation requirements in industries like water treatment and gas separation.</p></p>

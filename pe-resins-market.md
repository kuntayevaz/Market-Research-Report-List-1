LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) is a flexible plastic used in various packaging applications due to its low density and good clarity. LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) possesses both the flexibility of LDPE and the strength of HDPE, making it ideal for films, bags, and packaging materials that require high puncture resistance.

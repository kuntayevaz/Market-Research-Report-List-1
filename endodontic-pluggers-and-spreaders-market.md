<p><p>Endodontic pluggers and spreaders are dental instruments used in endodontic procedures to compact and shape filling materials inside the root canal. Pluggers are used to condense gutta-percha, a thermoplastic material used for root canal fillings, while spreaders are used to create space for the filling material. These instruments are essential in achieving successful root canal treatment.</p><p>Market analysis shows that the global endodontic pluggers and spreaders market is expected to experience significant growth in the forecast period. The market is driven by various factors such as the rising prevalence of dental diseases, increasing demand for minimally invasive procedures, and growing awareness about oral health. Additionally, advancements in dental technology and materials have improved the effectiveness of endodontic pluggers and spreaders, further propelling market growth.</p><p>The market is also witnessing various trends. One prominent trend is the increasing adoption of nickel-titanium (NiTi) alloy instruments. These instruments offer greater flexibility, enhanced durability, and improved resistance to cyclic fatigue compared to traditional stainless-steel instruments. NiTi alloy pluggers and spreaders are becoming the preferred choice among endodontists, contributing to the growth of the market.</p><p>Another trend is the integration of advanced features into endodontic instruments.

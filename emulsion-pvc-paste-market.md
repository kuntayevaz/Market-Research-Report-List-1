<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Emulsion PVC Paste market comprises two main types: the Micro Suspension Method and the Emulsion Method. The Micro Suspension Method involves blending PVC resin with other additives and a liquid medium to form particles suspended in a non-reactive liquid. This method offers advantages like high particle size control and good dispersion. On the other hand, the Emulsion Method involves the polymerization of vinyl chloride monomer with the help of an initiator to create an emulsion. This method offers advantages like high storage stability and ease of blending with various materials.</p></p>

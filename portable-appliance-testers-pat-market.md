<p><p>Portable Appliance Testers (PAT) are devices used to test the safety of electrical appliances. There are different market types within the PAT industry. Fail/Pass PAT Testing determines if an appliance meets safety standards. Earth bond Testing checks the integrity of the appliance's protective earth connection. Insulation Testing assesses the insulation resistance of the appliance. Battery Powered Testing verifies the performance of battery-operated appliances. Leakage/load Testing measures the amount of electric current leakage or load that an appliance produces. These market types cater to different safety requirements and help ensure electrical appliance safety.</p></p>

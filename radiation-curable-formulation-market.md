These technologies are extensively used in a variety of industries, including coatings, adhesives, and inks. UV curing offers faster curing times and is more suitable for thinner coatings, while EB curing provides deeper and more thorough curing, making it ideal for thicker materials. Both technologies offer enhanced efficiencies and environmental benefits.</p></p>

<p><p>Batter applicators are machines used in the food industry to coat products such as meat, fish, or vegetables with batter. Automatic batter applicators are fully automated machines that can apply batter to products without any human intervention. They are highly efficient and are suitable for large-scale production. On the other hand, semi-automatic batter applicators require some human involvement to operate the machine. They are typically used in smaller-scale production settings where some level of manual control is preferred.</p></p>

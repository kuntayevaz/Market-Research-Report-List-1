<p><p>The vehicle timing belt market is classified into three types: belt-in-oil, chains, and dry belts. Belt-in-oil timing belts are immersed in oil to reduce friction and noise, providing smooth operation. Chains are made of metal and are highly durable, suitable for heavy-duty vehicles. Dry belts are made of rubber and have a high grip, ensuring precise timing in engine operation. These different types cater to specific needs and preferences in the market, offering diverse options for vehicle manufacturers and consumers.</p></p>

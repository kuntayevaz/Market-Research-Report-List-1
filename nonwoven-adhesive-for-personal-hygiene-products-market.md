<p><p>The nonwoven adhesive market for personal hygiene products can be classified into two types: hot melt adhesive and water-based adhesives. Hot melt adhesive is a type of adhesive that is solid at room temperature but melts when heated, allowing it to be easily applied. It offers quick adhesion and has high cohesive strength. On the other hand, water-based adhesives are made from water, a polymer binder, and additives. They provide good wetting properties, are environmentally friendly, and have excellent adhesion to various substrates.</p></p>

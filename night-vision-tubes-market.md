<p><p>Night vision tubes are used in various applications to enhance visibility in low-light conditions. There are two main types of night vision tubes: uncooled type and cooled type. Uncooled type night vision tubes utilize thermal imaging technology to provide images based on heat emitted by objects, making them suitable for applications such as surveillance and firefighting. Cooled type night vision tubes, on the other hand, use a cooling mechanism to amplify incoming light and enhance image resolution, making them more suitable for military purposes such as sniper scopes and surveillance systems.</p></p>

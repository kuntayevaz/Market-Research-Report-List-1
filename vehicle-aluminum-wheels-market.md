<p><p>The Vehicle Aluminum Wheels market can be categorized into three types based on the manufacturing processes used. The Casting Process involves pouring molten aluminum into a mold to create a wheel shape. The Forging Process involves shaping solid aluminum billets by applying high pressure and heat. The Semi-Solid Die Forging Process combines elements of casting and forging by using a semi-solid aluminum alloy to create a wheel shape. These different manufacturing processes offer varying characteristics and benefits to the Vehicle Aluminum Wheels market.</p></p>

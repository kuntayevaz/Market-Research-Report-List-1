<p><p>The superhard cutting tool market has two main types: Polycrystalline Cubic Boron Nitride (PCBN) and Polycrystalline Diamond (PCD). PCBN tools consist of boron mixed with nitrogen, forming a durable material ideal for machining ferrous materials like cast iron and hardened steel. On the other hand, PCD tools are made up of multiple diamond particles, providing exceptional hardness and wear resistance, making them suitable for non-ferrous materials like aluminum and plastics. Both PCBN and PCD tools are highly sought after in the cutting tool market for their superior performance and longevity.</p></p>

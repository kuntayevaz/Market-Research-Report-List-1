Medium-range forecasting extends up to 5 to 7 days by utilizing advanced models and historical data. Long-range forecasting looks further ahead, up to several months or even a year, using statistical methods and climate data. These market types cater to different needs ranging from everyday planning to long-term climate trend analysis.</p></p>

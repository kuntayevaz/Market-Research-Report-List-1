<p><p>The multi-depth corrugated box market comprises three main types: single wall, double wall, and triple wall. Single wall boxes are made from one layer of corrugated board and are suitable for light to medium-weight products. Double wall boxes have two layers of corrugated board, providing increased strength and suitable for heavier products. Triple wall boxes, with three layers of corrugated board, offer the highest level of strength and are used for very heavy or fragile items. These different market types cater to varying needs and requirements, providing options for businesses with diverse packaging requirements.</p></p>

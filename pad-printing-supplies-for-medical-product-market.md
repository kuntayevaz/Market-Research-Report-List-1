<p><strong>The Pad Printing Supplies for Medical Product Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-based</li><li>UV Curing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are two main types of pad printing supplies used in the medical product market: solvent-based and UV curing. Solvent-based supplies consist of inks that contain solvents, which evaporate during the printing process, leaving the print on the product. These inks are suitable for printing on a wide range of materials. On the other hand, UV curing supplies utilize inks that are exposed to UV light, resulting in a rapid drying and hardening process. These inks are effective for printing on heat-sensitive materials and offer excellent resistance to abrasion and chemicals.</p></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The High Voltage Motor Pt100 Temperature Sensor market can be categorized into two types: bearing type and stator embedded type. </p><p>Bearing type sensors are installed near the bearings of the high voltage motor. These sensors detect the temperature of the bearings, helping to prevent overheating and potential damage. </p><p>On the other hand, stator embedded type sensors are integrated within the stator windings of the motor. These sensors monitor the temperature of the stator windings, ensuring efficient operation and preventing any thermal issues. </p><p>Both types of sensors are crucial in maintaining the optimal temperature of the high voltage motor for its reliable and safe operation.</p></p>

<p><strong>The RO Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Composite Membranes</li><li>Asymmetric Membranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RO membrane market consists of two main types: composite membranes and asymmetric membranes. Composite membranes are made up of multiple layers, including a thin polyamide layer that provides excellent salt rejection and a thicker polyester support layer that enhances durability. On the other hand, asymmetric membranes have a single dense skin layer that allows for higher water flow and lower energy consumption. Both types of membranes are widely used in water treatment processes, providing efficient separation of contaminants from water sources.</p></p>

<p><p>The NdFeB magnets market can be divided into two main types: sintered NdFeB magnets and bonded NdFeB magnets. Sintered NdFeB magnets are made through a process of powder metallurgy, where the raw materials are mixed, pressed, and sintered at high temperatures to form the final product. These magnets have high magnetic properties and are widely used in various industries like automotive, electronics, and renewable energy. On the other hand, bonded NdFeB magnets are manufactured by mixing magnetic powders with a binder, typically a plastic or rubber material. While these magnets have lower magnetic properties compared to sintered NdFeB magnets, they offer more design flexibility and are commonly used in smaller or more complex applications.</p></p>

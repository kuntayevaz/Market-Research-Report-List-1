<p><p>Engineered Quartz (E-Quartz) Countertops are a popular choice for kitchen and bathroom surfaces due to their durability, low maintenance, and aesthetic appeal. Two types of E-Quartz countertops are Phaneromer Quartz and Cryptocrystalline Quartz. Phaneromer Quartz is composed of larger quartz particles, giving it a more granular appearance, while Cryptocrystalline Quartz consists of smaller particles, resulting in a smoother and more uniform surface. Both types offer the advantages of resistance to scratches, stains, and heat, making them an ideal option for stylish and long-lasting countertops.</p></p>

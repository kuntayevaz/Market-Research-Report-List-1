<p><p>The sterile diaphragm valve market encompasses several types, each distinguished by the method used to operate the valve. Manual valves are manually controlled, requiring physical interaction for operation. Pneumatic valves are driven by compressed air, which allows for automatic control. Electric valves are powered electronically, enabling remote control and automation. Lastly, other market segments may include valves that utilize alternative methods such as hydraulic or solenoid control. These different types cater to diverse industries and applications, providing flexibility and options for the users' specific needs.</p></p>

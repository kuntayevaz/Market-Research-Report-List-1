<p><p>The high-performance insulation market includes various types of insulation materials such as aerogel, vacuum insulation panel (VIP), fiberglass, ceramic fiber, and high-performance foam. Aerogel is a lightweight, nanoporous material with excellent thermal properties. VIPs consist of a core material encapsulated in a vacuum, providing extremely low thermal conductivity. Fiberglass is made from fine glass fibers and is widely used for its thermal insulation properties. Ceramic fiber insulation is made from inorganic fibers and is known for its high-temperature resistance. High-performance foam offers excellent insulation performance due to its low thermal conductivity and high density.</p></p>

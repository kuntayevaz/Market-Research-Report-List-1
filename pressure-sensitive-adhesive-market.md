<p><p>The pressure-sensitive adhesive market consists of various types: water-based, solvent-based, hot melt, radiation, and other markets. Water-based adhesives make use of water as a carrier, offering low toxicity and easy clean-up. Solvent-based adhesives contain organic solvents and provide high initial adhesion and versatility for different substrates. Hot melt adhesives are solid at room temperature and melt upon heating, offering fast setting and good bonding. Radiation-curable adhesives offer quick curing through UV or electron beam exposure. Lastly, other adhesive markets include products like acrylic-based and silicone-based adhesives, catering to specific application needs.</p></p>

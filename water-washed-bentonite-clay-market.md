<p><p>The water-washed bentonite clay market is divided into three types: sodium bentonite, calcium bentonite, and potassium bentonite. Sodium bentonite is known for its high swelling capacity and is widely used in drilling muds and civil engineering applications. Calcium bentonite has a lower swelling capacity but is valued for its ability to absorb toxins, making it suitable for use in animal feed additives and cosmetics. Potassium bentonite has similar properties to sodium bentonite and finds applications in the pharmaceutical and personal care industries.</p></p>

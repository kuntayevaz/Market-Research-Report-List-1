<p><p>The sheet membrane market is categorized into two types: Type I and Type II. Type I membranes are primarily used for waterproofing applications. They possess excellent resistance to weathering, making them suitable for exterior uses. On the other hand, Type II membranes are commonly utilized in roofing applications. They have high tensile strength and elongation properties, which enhance their durability and ability to withstand harsh weather conditions. Both types of membranes play significant roles in various construction projects, ensuring water tightness and protection against moisture intrusion.</p></p>

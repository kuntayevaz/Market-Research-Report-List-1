<p><p>The semi-open breathing circuit market comprises two types: disposable and reusable circuits. Disposable circuits are designed for single-use only, providing a sanitary and convenient option for patients. These circuits are disposed of after each use, reducing the risk of cross-contamination and the need for cleaning and sterilization. Reusable circuits, on the other hand, are designed to be cleaned and sterilized for multiple uses. While they require more maintenance and cleaning steps, they can be cost-effective in the long run and are more environmentally friendly since they generate less waste.</p></p>

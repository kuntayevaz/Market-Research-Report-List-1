<p><p>The automotive radar market consists of three types of radar sensors: long range, medium range, and short range. Long range radar sensors are typically used for advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS) and autonomous driving applications. They can detect objects up to several hundred meters away. Medium range radar sensors are commonly used for mid-level ADAS features such as blind spot detection and cross traffic alert. They can detect objects at a range of tens to hundreds of meters. Short range radar sensors are primarily used for parking assistance and collision avoidance, detecting objects within a range of a few meters.</p></p>

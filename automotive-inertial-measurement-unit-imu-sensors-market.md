<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Automotive Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU) Sensors market consists of two main types: MEMS gyroscope-based IMUs and FOG-based IMUs. MEMS gyroscope-based IMUs utilize Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems (MEMS) technology to measure rotational motion accurately. These IMUs are cost-effective, compact, and widely used in automotive applications. On the other hand, FOG-based IMUs use Fiber Optic Gyroscopes (FOG) to measure rotational motion. FOG-based IMUs offer higher accuracy and stability compared to MEMS-based IMUs, making them suitable for demanding applications in automotive industries where precise motion detection is critical.</p></p>

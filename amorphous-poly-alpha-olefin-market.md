<p><p>Amorphous Poly Alpha Olefin (APAO) is a type of thermoplastic olefin copolymer produced by the copolymerization of alpha-olefins with an amorphous polyolefin resin. APAO is used in packaging materials due to its excellent sealing properties, resistance to moisture, and high clarity.</p><p>Furthermore, the rising awareness regarding environmentally friendly products and the shift towards sustainable packaging materials are anticipated to further boost the demand for APAO.

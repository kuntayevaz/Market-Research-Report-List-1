<p><p>The invisible orthodontics products market includes three primary types: ceramic braces, clear aligners, and lingual braces. Lingual braces are similar to traditional metal braces but are placed on the inside surface of the teeth, effectively hiding them from view. These options provide patients with more aesthetically pleasing alternatives to traditional braces while achieving the desired orthodontic results.</p></p>

They are used in various applications such as packaging and agricultural films, promising lower environmental impact. Non-compostable films, on the other hand, are also bio-based but do not degrade completely in composting conditions. These films provide durability and barrier properties and find applications in industries like food packaging and medical sectors.</p></p>

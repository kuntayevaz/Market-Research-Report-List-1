<p><p>Automatic Power Factor Controller (APFC) is a system used to regulate and maintain the power factor of electrical systems. Active APFCs incorporate active components like thyristors and microprocessors, allowing them to dynamically adjust power factor in real-time. These controllers are efficient and precise but are more expensive. On the other hand, passive APFCs use fixed components such as capacitors and reactors. They are cheaper but less flexible as they cannot adjust the power factor instantly. The market for APFCs consists of both active and passive types, catering to different budget and requirement needs.</p></p>

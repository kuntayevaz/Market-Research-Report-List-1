<p><p>The Automotive Electric Scroll Compressors market comprises two main types: Hermetic Scroll Compressors and Semi-hermetic Scroll Compressors. Hermetic Scroll Compressors are sealed units where both the motor and compressor are enclosed, helping to prevent any external contamination. These compressors are popular in automotive applications due to their compact size and efficiency. On the other hand, Semi-hermetic Scroll Compressors have a removable compressor cover, enabling easier repair and maintenance. This type offers flexibility and is often utilized in larger automotive systems where repairability is important.</p></p>

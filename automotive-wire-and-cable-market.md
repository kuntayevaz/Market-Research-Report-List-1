<p><p>The automotive wire and cable market can be categorized into three types: copper core, aluminum core, and others. Copper core wires are the most common and widely used due to their excellent conductivity, durability, and resistance to corrosion. Aluminum core wires are an alternative to copper, offering similar electrical performance at a lower cost, but are less robust and require larger wire diameters. The "others" category includes specialty wires and cables, such as fiber-optic cables, coaxial cables, and high-temperature wires, that are used for specific applications in vehicles.</p></p>

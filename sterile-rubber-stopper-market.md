<p><p>The sterile rubber stopper market comprises different types, including injection stoppers, infusion stoppers, and lyophilization stoppers. Injection stoppers are used in pharmaceutical packaging to ensure airtight sealing of vials during drug administration. Infusion stoppers are designed for the secure closure of infusion bottles, preserving drug efficacy and preventing contamination. Lyophilization stoppers are used in freeze-drying processes to maintain the stability and integrity of the lyophilized product. All these stoppers play a crucial role in maintaining safety, reliability, and quality standards in the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>

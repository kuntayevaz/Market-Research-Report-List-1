<p><p>High purity photoacid generator (PAG) market types include photo-radical initiators and photo-cationic initiators. Photo-radical initiators are compounds that produce radical species when exposed to light, initiating polymerization reactions. They are commonly used in applications such as 3D printing and lithography. Photo-cationic initiators, on the other hand, generate cationic species upon light exposure, enabling the initiation of polymerization reactions in specific areas. They find applications in areas like microelectronics and manufacturing. Both types of PAGs are essential for various industries that rely on light-induced polymerization processes.</p></p>

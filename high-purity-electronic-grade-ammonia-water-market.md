SLSI refers to Super-Large-Scale Integration, which denotes less advanced chips but still having a high transistor density. XLSI stands for eXtra-Large-Scale Integration and refers to smaller-scale microchips. XXLSI represents microchips with even lower integration levels. These markets require different levels of purity in Ammonia Water to ensure the desired quality for manufacturing processes.</p></p>

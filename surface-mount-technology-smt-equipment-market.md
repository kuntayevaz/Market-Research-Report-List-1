The increasing demand for smaller and more efficient electronic devices, such as smartphones, tablets, and wearable devices, is driving the growth of the SMT equipment market. IoT devices require compact and reliable electronic components, and SMT technology offers the necessary capabilities for their manufacturing.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in SMT equipment technology, such as the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning, are expected to enhance the productivity and efficiency of the assembly process.

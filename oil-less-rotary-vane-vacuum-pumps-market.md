<p><p>The oil-less rotary vane vacuum pumps market consists of two types: single stage rotary vane vacuum pumps and two stage rotary vane vacuum pumps. Single stage pumps have one set of vanes that create the vacuum by rotating against the housing, while two stage pumps have two sets of vanes that work in tandem to produce a deeper vacuum. Both types eliminate the need for oil lubrication, making them ideal for applications where oil contamination is a concern, such as in food processing or semiconductor industries.</p></p>

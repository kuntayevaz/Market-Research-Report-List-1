<p><p>The Vehicle Passive Keyless Entry System market can be categorized into two types: PIC Based and ARM Based. PIC Based systems utilize microcontrollers called Programmable Interface Controllers, which are cost-effective and efficient for small-scale applications. On the other hand, ARM Based systems use Advanced RISC Machines microprocessors that offer high performance and flexibility, suitable for complex and sophisticated applications. Both types serve the same purpose of allowing vehicle owners to access and start their vehicles without physically using a key, but differ in terms of technology and capabilities.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Agricultural Activator Adjuvants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surfactants</li><li>Oil-based Adjuvants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Agricultural activator adjuvants are substances used in agricultural practices to enhance the effectiveness of agrochemicals. Surfactants are one type of adjuvant that help in reducing surface tension, improving wetting, spreading, and absorption of the agrochemical on plant surfaces. They aid in ensuring better coverage, increasing overall efficiency and efficacy. Oil-based adjuvants, on the other hand, consist of oils or petroleum-derived compounds that act as spreading agents, ensuring better adhesion of the agrochemical to plant surfaces. They also enhance rainfastness, reducing the risk of wash-off. Both surfactants and oil-based adjuvants play vital roles in optimizing the performance of agrochemicals in agricultural applications.</p></p>

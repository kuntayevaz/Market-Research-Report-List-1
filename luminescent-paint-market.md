<p><p>Luminescent paint refers to a type of paint that emits light after being charged or exposed to a light source. There are three main types of luminescent paint on the market. Fluorescent paint absorbs ultraviolet light and emits visible light instantly. Phosphorescent paint absorbs and stores energy from a light source, gradually releasing it over time in darkness. Radio luminescent paint uses radioluminescent materials that emit light when excited by ionizing radiation. These different types offer various applications in industries such as safety, art, and entertainment.</p></p>

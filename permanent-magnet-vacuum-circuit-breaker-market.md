<p><p>The Permanent Magnet Vacuum Circuit Breaker (PMVCB) market consists of two types: Monostable and Bistable. Monostable PMVCBs require a continuous supply of energy to maintain the circuit breaker's open or closed state. These are commonly used in applications where the circuit breaker's state needs to be frequently changed. On the other hand, Bistable PMVCBs can hold their open or closed state without any additional energy input. They are suitable for applications where a stable state is required for extended periods, eliminating the need for continuous energy supply.</p></p>

<p><p>The travel mobility scooter market can be classified into two types: Class 2 and Class 3 scooters. Class 2 scooters are intended for indoor use and limited outdoor use on level ground. They have a lower speed range and are less stable than Class 3 scooters. Class 3 scooters, on the other hand, are designed for both indoor and outdoor use. They have a higher maximum speed and better stability due to enhanced suspension and larger wheels. This classification helps consumers choose the appropriate scooter based on their intended usage and mobility requirements.</p></p>

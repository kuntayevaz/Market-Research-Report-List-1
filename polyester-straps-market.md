<p><p>Polyester straps are used in various industries for securing and packaging goods. There are two main types of polyester straps: Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) straps and Polypropylene (PP) straps. PET straps are made from a strong, durable, and high-tensile material. They are commonly used for heavy-duty applications such as strapping pallets and securing large loads. On the other hand, PP straps are more economical and suitable for lighter loads. They offer good elongation and flexibility, making them ideal for smaller packages and bundling items together.</p></p>

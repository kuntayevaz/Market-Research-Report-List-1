<p><p>Static Var Compensators (SVCs) are devices used in power systems to regulate and stabilize voltage levels. They come in two main market types: Thyristor-Based and Magnetically Controlled Reactor (MCR)-Based. Thyristor-Based SVCs use thyristors to control the power flow and voltage levels, ensuring efficient and fast compensation. On the other hand, MCR-Based SVCs utilize magnetically controlled reactors to regulate voltage levels, providing a cost-effective solution for smaller power systems. Both types offer effective voltage control and stability, catering to different market requirements.</p></p>

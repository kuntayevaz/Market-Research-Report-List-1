<p><p>PVC adhesive tapes can be classified into two types: Scotch Tape and Opaque Tape. Scotch Tape is a transparent PVC adhesive tape widely used for sealing, mounting, and repairing purposes. It is known for its strong adhesive properties and clarity. On the other hand, Opaque Tape is an opaque-colored PVC adhesive tape used primarily for packaging and covering purposes where transparency is not required. Both types serve different applications but are commonly utilized in various industries for their reliable adhesive strength and durability.</p></p>

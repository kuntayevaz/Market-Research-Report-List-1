<p><p>Asset management market types can be categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II markets. Type I markets refer to centralized asset management systems where a single entity manages all assets on behalf of investors. This type provides convenience and efficiency as investors can benefit from professional management. On the other hand, Type II markets involve decentralized asset management, allowing investors to have more control over their investments and choose from a range of asset managers. This type offers more flexibility and customization options for investors.</p></p>

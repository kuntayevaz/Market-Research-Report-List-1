<p><p>The Aramid Fiber Reinforced Plastic (AFRP) market consists of two main types: thermoplastic and thermoset. Thermoplastic AFRP is a type of plastic material that can be melted and re-molded multiple times without undergoing a significant change in its properties. On the other hand, thermoset AFRP is a type of plastic material that, once cured or hardened through a chemical reaction, becomes irreversibly solid and cannot be re-melted or re-molded. Both thermoplastic and thermoset AFRP have their unique advantages and applications within various industries.</p></p>

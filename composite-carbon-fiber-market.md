<p><p>The composite carbon fiber market comprises three main types: continuous carbon fiber, long carbon fiber, and short carbon fiber. Continuous carbon fiber is characterized by a continuous filament structure, providing superior strength and stiffness. Long carbon fiber features fibers with a length of more than 1 cm, offering enhanced mechanical properties. Short carbon fiber, on the other hand, consists of fibers typically less than 0.8 cm in length, which provide improved impact resistance and processing ease. These diverse types cater to various applications in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and sports equipment, providing tailored solutions for specific requirements.</p></p>

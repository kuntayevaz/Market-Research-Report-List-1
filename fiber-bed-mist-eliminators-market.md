<p><p>Fiber bed mist eliminators are used in various industries to remove mist and droplets from gas streams. They can be classified into high-speed and low-speed market types. High-speed mist eliminators are designed for applications where gas velocities are high, typically above 5 m/s. They are suitable for removing fine droplets and mist in fast-moving gas streams. On the other hand, low-speed mist eliminators are designed for lower gas velocities, below 5 m/s. They are effective in capturing larger droplets and mist in slower gas streams.</p></p>

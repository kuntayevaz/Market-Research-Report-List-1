<p><p>Automotive Electronic Braking System (EBS) refers to a technology used in vehicles to enhance braking efficiency and safety. The market types of EBS are disc brake and drum brake. Disc brake system employs a rotor and caliper to provide effective braking, offering better heat dissipation and stopping power. On the other hand, the drum brake system operates using brake shoes and drums, offering a cost-effective solution but less effective heat dissipation. Both options cater to different vehicle requirements, with disc brakes being more popular in modern vehicles due to better performance and reduced maintenance needs.</p></p>

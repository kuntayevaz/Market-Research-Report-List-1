<p><p>Water-based resin, also known as waterborne resin, is a type of resin that is dispersed in water instead of a solvent. It offers various advantages such as low emissions of volatile organic compounds (VOCs), easy clean-up with water, and reduced flammability compared to solvent-based resins. It is widely used in coatings, adhesives, sealants, and other applications across industries such as construction, automotive, packaging, and furniture.</p><p>The water-based resin market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The growing focus on sustainable and environmentally friendly products, stringent regulations regarding VOC emissions, and the increasing demand for water-based coatings and adhesives are driving the market growth.

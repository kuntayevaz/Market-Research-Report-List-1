<p><p>Neurofeedback systems are used to monitor and train brain activity for various applications. Slow Cortical Potential Neurofeedback (SCP-NF) focuses on training slow brain waves linked to attention and relaxation. Low-energy Neurofeedback System (LENS) employs a gentle radio frequency to stimulate the brain and regulate dysregulated brainwave patterns. Hemoencephalographic (HEG) neurofeedback involves training prefrontal cortex activity using near-infrared light. Other neurofeedback systems encompass a range of approaches like Z-Score neurofeedback and Alpha-Theta neurofeedback. These systems offer personalized brain training solutions for conditions such as ADHD, anxiety, insomnia, and cognitive enhancement.</p></p>

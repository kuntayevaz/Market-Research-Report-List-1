<p><p>The Diesel Common Rail Direct Injection System market consists of two main types: Solenoid Type and Piezo Type. </p><p>The Solenoid Type operates by using a solenoid to control the fuel injector, allowing fuel to be injected into the engine at precise intervals. This type is known for its reliability and cost-effectiveness. </p><p>On the other hand, the Piezo Type utilizes a piezoelectric crystal to control the fuel injector, ensuring extremely precise fuel delivery. This type offers superior fuel efficiency and reduced emissions, making it a popular choice for high-performance and environmentally-friendly vehicles.</p></p>

<p><p>The reinforcement fiber market for wind turbine blades comprises two main types: glass fiber and carbon fiber. Glass fiber is widely used in wind turbine blade manufacturing due to its low cost, high strength, and lightweight properties. It is the most commonly used reinforcement material in the industry. On the other hand, carbon fiber offers superior mechanical properties, including high strength and stiffness, as well as excellent fatigue resistance.

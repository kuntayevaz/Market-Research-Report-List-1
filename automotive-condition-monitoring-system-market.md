<p><p>The Automotive Condition Monitoring System market includes two types of systems: Cloud System and Local System. The Cloud System utilizes the cloud technology to gather, store, and analyze large amounts of data from various automotive components for monitoring purposes. It provides real-time updates and remote access, enabling efficient maintenance and decision-making. On the other hand, the Local System works offline within the vehicle, collecting and analyzing data locally. It offers real-time monitoring without relying on internet connectivity but may require manual intervention for maintenance and reporting. Both systems offer unique benefits depending on the user's requirements and preferences.</p></p>

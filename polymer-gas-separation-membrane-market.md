<p><p>The Polymer Gas Separation Membrane Market can be classified into three types - hollow fiber, spiral wound, and others. Hollow fiber membranes consist of small tubes with a porous structure, allowing gas molecules to pass through while blocking contaminants. Spiral wound membranes are composed of a flat sheet folded into a spiral structure, making them more compact and efficient. The "other" category includes various types of polymer membranes, such as ceramic composites or mixed matrix membranes, which offer unique separation capabilities. These different types cater to diverse industrial gas separation applications.</p></p>

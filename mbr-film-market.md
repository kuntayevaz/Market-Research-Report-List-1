<p><p>The MBR (Membrane Bioreactor) film market encompasses various types of films that are used in wastewater treatment processes. Flat film, as the name suggests, is a planar membrane used extensively in MBR systems. Tube film refers to membranes in the form of small hollow tubes, providing a high surface area for filtration. Hollow fiber membranes are long, thin, and hollow, offering efficient filtration due to their porous structure. The "Other" market category includes specialized MBR films, such as spiral wound or multichannel designs, that cater to specific applications, providing unique benefits or advantages.</p></p>

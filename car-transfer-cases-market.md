Gear driven transfer cases use a set of gears to transfer power, while chain driven transfer cases use a chain to do the same. Both types have their advantages and disadvantages. Gear driven transfer cases are more durable and provide better fuel efficiency, while chain driven transfer cases are quieter and offer smoother operation.</p></p>

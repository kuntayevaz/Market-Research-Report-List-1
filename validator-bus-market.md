<p><strong>The Validator Bus Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-station Validator</li><li>Multi-Station Validator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The validator bus market includes two types: one-station validators and multi-station validators. One-station validators are designed for buses that have only one place where passengers can enter and pay, such as a front door. These validators help to ensure a smooth and efficient payment process for single-entry buses. On the other hand, multi-station validators are suitable for buses that have multiple entry points, such as front and rear doors. These validators enable passengers to pay at any entrance, enhancing convenience and reducing crowding during boarding.</p></p>

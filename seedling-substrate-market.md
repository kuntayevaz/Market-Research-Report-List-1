<p><p>The seedling substrate market consists of various types of materials used for the germination and growth of plants. Soilless mixes are substrates that do not contain any soil and are made up of organic matter such as coco coir or peat moss blended with other components. Composts are organic materials that have undergone decomposition and are rich in nutrients. Gravel is a type of inorganic substrate composed of small stones or pebbles. Inert media include materials like rockwool or perlite that do not contain any nutrients. Other substrates can include sand or vermiculite, providing different options for plant growth.</p></p>

<p><p>Functional polymer sponge is a type of material that is widely used in industries for various applications. It can be classified into different types based on its chemical composition. Amide sponge, composed of amide groups, offers high thermal stability and toughness. Ether sponge, with ether linkages, exhibits excellent resistance to chemicals and low water absorption. Esters sponge, made of ester groups, provides good resistance to UV radiation and aging. Other types include sponges with different functional groups, each having unique properties and applications. Overall, functional polymer sponges offer versatile options for industries seeking specific material characteristics.</p></p>

<p><p>The Industrial Traction Equipment market consists of three main types: Traction Transformer, Traction Motor, and Traction Converter. </p><p>- Traction Transformers are devices that change the voltage level needed for efficiently transmitting electricity from the power source to the train's motor.</p><p>- Traction Motors are responsible for converting electrical energy received from the transformer into mechanical energy, enabling the train to move.</p><p>- Traction Converters, on the other hand, convert electrical energy from overhead lines or third rails into the correct voltage and frequency required by the train's motor. Overall, these components work together to facilitate efficient and reliable operation of trains.</p></p>

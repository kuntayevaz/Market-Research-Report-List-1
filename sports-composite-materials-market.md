<p><p>Sports composite materials are commonly used in the sports industry due to their lightweight and high-performance properties. Carbon fiber composite materials are made from carbon fibers embedded in a matrix material, resulting in high strength and stiffness. They are widely used in applications like cycling and tennis rackets. On the other hand, glass fiber composite materials are made from glass fibers embedded in a matrix material, offering good flexibility and impact resistance. They are commonly used in sports equipment such as surfboards and hockey sticks. Both materials play a crucial role in enhancing sports performance and durability.</p></p>

<p><p>The ski and snowboard wax market can be classified into two main types: glide wax and grip wax. Glide wax is used to reduce friction between the equipment and the snow, allowing for smoother and faster gliding over the snow surface. On the other hand, grip wax is applied to the base of the equipment to enhance traction and grip on icy or hard-packed snow, preventing slippage during turns or uphill climbs. These two types of wax cater to different needs of skiers and snowboarders, ensuring optimal performance in various snow conditions.</p></p>

<p><p>The Blocked Isocyanate market can be categorized into two types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based Blocked Isocyanate refers to a type of product where the isocyanate is chemically blocked with a water-soluble blocking agent. This makes it safer and easier to handle and reduces the release of harmful isocyanates. On the other hand, solvent-based Blocked Isocyanate involves using a solvent as the blocking agent. This type of product is typically used in applications that require faster curing and better adhesion properties.</p></p>

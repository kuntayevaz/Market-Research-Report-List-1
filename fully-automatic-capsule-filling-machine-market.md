<p><p>The fully-automatic capsule filling machine market includes two types: the Mechanical Vibration Filling Machine and the Compression Filling Machine. </p><p>The Mechanical Vibration Filling Machine utilizes vibrations to evenly distribute the powder into the capsules while ensuring a consistent fill level. This type of machine is ideal for powders with poor flow properties or those prone to separation. </p><p>On the other hand, the Compression Filling Machine compresses the powder into tablets, which are then loaded into the capsules. This method is suitable for products that require high compression or have specific hardness requirements. Both types provide efficient and automated solutions for capsule filling in various industries.</p></p>

<p><p>The aerogel insulation market is categorized into three types: Silica, Carbon, and Oxides. Silica aerogel is composed of amorphous silica and offers excellent thermal insulation properties. Carbon aerogel is made from carbon materials and has high thermal conductivity, making it suitable for applications requiring superior insulation. Oxide aerogel includes various types like alumina, titania, and zirconia, each offering unique thermal insulation characteristics. These aerogel types are widely used in industries like construction, oil and gas, automotive, and aerospace for their lightweight, low thermal conductivity, and high insulation properties.</p></p>

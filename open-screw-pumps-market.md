<p><p>Open screw pumps are a type of positive displacement pump used for various industrial applications. Single-screw pumps consist of a rotating screw inside a stationary casing, creating a continuous flow by pushing the fluid forward. They are suitable for low viscosity liquids and are known for their simplicity and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, multi-screw pumps have two or more intermeshing screws, which generate higher flow rates and can handle higher viscosity fluids. They provide efficient pumping for various industries, including oil and gas, chemical, and food processing.</p></p>

<p><p>The plastic seals market is categorized into three types based on their durability and strength: heavy duty, medium duty, and light duty seals. Heavy duty seals are specifically designed for highly secure applications, such as shipping containers or airline cargo, as they provide maximum strength and tamper-evidence. Medium duty seals offer a moderate level of security and are commonly used in industries like transportation and logistics. Light duty seals are the least strong and are suitable for applications that require basic tamper-evidence, such as meter sealing or small packaging.</p></p>

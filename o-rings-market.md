<p><p>O-Rings, also known as mechanical gaskets, are circular rubber seals with a round cross-section. They are commonly used to prevent leakage of fluids and gases in various industrial applications. O-Rings form a seal between two surfaces and can withstand both high and low temperatures, making them suitable for use in a wide range of industries such as automotive, aerospace, manufacturing, oil and gas, and pharmaceuticals.</p><p>The current outlook of the O-Rings market is positive and is expected to experience steady growth in the coming years. The rising need for efficient sealing solutions to prevent leaks and ensure operational efficiency is also driving the demand for O-Rings.</p><p>Additionally, the ongoing technological advancements in rubber materials and manufacturing techniques are further expected to boost the growth of the O-Rings market.

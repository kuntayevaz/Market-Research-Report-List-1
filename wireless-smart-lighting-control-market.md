Bluetooth allows for short-range communication between devices. Z-Wave is another wireless technology that focuses on home automation devices. Lastly, EnOcean employs energy harvesting technology, eliminating the need for battery or wired power supply. These technologies provide different options for consumers to control their lighting systems wirelessly.</p></p>

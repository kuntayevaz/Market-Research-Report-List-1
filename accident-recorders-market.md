<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Dual Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Accident recorders are devices used to capture and store data related to vehicular accidents. Single channel accident recorders are equipped with one camera and can record footage of the road ahead. They are commonly used in vehicles where the risk of accidents is relatively low. Dual channel accident recorders, on the other hand, have two cameras and can record footage both from the front and rear of the vehicle. These recorders are typically used in commercial vehicles or in situations where recording both views is necessary for comprehensive accident documentation.</p></p>

<p><p>Gene therapy products refer to the use of genes to treat or prevent diseases by altering the genetic material within living cells. Overall, the future of gene therapy products appears promising, revolutionizing the field of medicine and offering hope for patients with previously incurable diseases.</p></p>

<p><p>Gene therapy products are innovative treatments designed to address genetic disorders. There are two types of gene therapy markets: ex vivo and in vivo. Ex vivo gene therapy involves modifying the patient's cells outside the body and then re-introducing them back into the patient. In contrast, in vivo gene therapy directly delivers the therapy to the patient's body. Both approaches aim to introduce functional genes, correct mutations, or regulate gene expression to address diseases at the genetic level and offer potential long-term benefits to patients.</p></p>

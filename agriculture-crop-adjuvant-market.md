Oil adjuvants improve the efficiency of agricultural chemicals by helping them penetrate plant tissues. Surfactants reduce the surface tension of water and increase the wetting and spreading ability of chemicals. Utility adjuvants are versatile and can enhance the effectiveness of pesticides by improving their compatibility, stability, or reducing drift.</p></p>

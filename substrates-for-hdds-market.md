<p><p>The substrates for HDDs, namely the Glass Substrates and Aluminum Substrate markets, are essential components in the manufacturing of hard disk drives. Glass substrates are thin plates made of glass used to support the structure of the HDDs. They provide stability, heat resistance, and excellent flatness needed for the precise operations of the drive. On the other hand, Aluminum substrates are thin sheets made of aluminum that offer durability, electrical conductivity, and heat dissipation properties required for the HDD's functionality. Both substrates play crucial roles in ensuring the overall performance and reliability of HDDs.</p></p>

<p><h1>Onboard Dashboard Camera Market Growth, Market Trends, COVID-19 Impact, and Forecasts for period from 2023 - 2030</h1></p><p><strong>Onboard Dashboard Camera Market Analysis and Latest Trends</strong></p>
<p><p>An onboard dashboard camera, also known as a dashcam, is a small video recording device that is mounted on the dashboard or windscreen of a vehicle to capture video footage of the road ahead. It is designed to continuously record the view through the vehicle's front windshield and sometimes also the rear or sides of the vehicle. Dashcams are gaining popularity among drivers as they provide evidence in the event of accidents, help prevent insurance fraud, and improve driver behavior.</p><p>The onboard dashboard camera market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue expanding at a CAGR of 10.5% during the forecast period. The market analysis suggests that the growing concern for road safety and increasing adoption of advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS) are the primary factors driving the market growth. Governments and regulatory bodies in several countries are also encouraging the use of dashcams to improve road safety and hold drivers accountable for their actions.</p><p>The market is also witnessing a trend of integration of dashcams with other advanced technologies such as GPS navigation systems, collision avoidance systems, and cloud connectivity. This integration helps in providing additional features like real-time vehicle tracking, alerts for lane departure and potential collisions, and remote access to recorded footage. Such advanced functionalities are attracting consumers and contributing to the growth of the onboard dashboard camera market.</p><p>Furthermore, the increasing affordability of dashcams and the availability of a wide range of models with varying features and price points have made them more accessible to a larger consumer base. Additionally, the rising popularity of ride-hailing services, where onboard cameras are essential for the safety of the driver and passengers, contributes to the market growth.</p><p>In conclusion, the onboard dashboard camera market is expected to witness substantial growth in the coming years, driven by factors such as increasing road safety concerns, adoption of ADAS, integration with advanced technologies, affordability, and the rise of ride-hailing services.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Multi-Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The onboard dashboard camera market is categorized into two types: single channel and multi-channel. Single channel dashboard cameras consist of a single camera unit that records the footage in front of the vehicle. They are suitable for individual drivers who primarily want to monitor road conditions and capture road incidents. On the other hand, multi-channel dashboard cameras come with multiple camera units that record footage from multiple angles, both on the front and rear of the vehicle. This type of camera is ideal for commercial vehicles or for those who require comprehensive surveillance of their surroundings.</p></p>

<p><p>PTFE Composite Film is available in two types, namely Hydrophobic and Hydrophilic. Hydrophobic PTFE Composite Film repels water and other liquids due to its non-porous surface, making it suitable for applications where water resistance is required. On the other hand, Hydrophilic PTFE Composite Film attracts and absorbs water, allowing it to be used for applications requiring liquid absorption and filtration. Both types of film find applications in various industries, including automotive, electronics, textiles, and healthcare, based on their specific properties and functionalities.</p></p>

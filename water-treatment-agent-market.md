<p><p>The water treatment agent market can be categorized into different types based on their functions. Coagulants and flocculants are substances that are used to remove suspended particles from water. Corrosion inhibitors are used to prevent the deterioration of water treatment equipment due to corrosion. Scale inhibitors are used to prevent the formation of mineral deposits in water systems. Biocides and disinfectants are used to kill harmful microorganisms in water. Chelating agents are used to remove heavy metals from water. Anti-foaming agents are used to control foam formation. pH adjusters and stabilizers are used to maintain the desired pH level of water. There are also other types of water treatment agents available in the market.</p></p>

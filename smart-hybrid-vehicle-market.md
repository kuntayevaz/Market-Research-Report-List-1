<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Smart Hybrid Vehicle Market comprises two types of technologies: the Belt-integrated Starter Generator (BiSG) and the Crankshaft-integrated Motor Generator. The BiSG technology is a small electric motor that is connected to the engine's crankshaft via a belt. It assists the engine by providing additional power during acceleration and recharges the vehicle's battery during deceleration. On the other hand, the Crankshaft-integrated Motor Generator is a larger electric motor that is directly connected to the engine's crankshaft. It not only assists in accelerating the vehicle but also acts as a generator to recharge the battery.</p></p>

<p><p>The corrugating board/cardboard market includes various types based on the number of corrugated layers. Single corrugated board consists of one corrugated layer and two flat layers, making it suitable for lightweight packaging. Double corrugated board has two corrugated layers and three flat layers, providing enhanced strength for medium-weight packaging. Triple corrugated board offers the highest level of strength with three corrugated layers and four flat layers, making it ideal for heavy-duty packaging. Additionally, there are other specialized types in the market to cater to specific packaging requirements.</p></p>

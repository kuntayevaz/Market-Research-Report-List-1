<p><p>A full-size C-arm is a medical imaging device that is used for fluoroscopic imaging during surgical procedures. It consists of a large C-shaped arm that houses an X-ray generator and image intensifier or flat-panel detector. This device can be easily maneuvered around a patient to capture real-time images, making it a valuable tool for various medical procedures such as orthopedic surgeries, vascular interventions, and cardiac procedures.</p><p>The future outlook of the full-size C-arm market appears to be promising. Advancements in technology, such as the integration of 3D imaging capabilities, are expected to drive market growth. The ability to provide high-resolution images and real-time visualization during surgeries is crucial for better patient outcomes. Additionally, the increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, a rising geriatric population, and the growing demand for minimally invasive surgeries are anticipated to propel market growth.</p><p>The current outlook of the full-size C-arm market is also positive.

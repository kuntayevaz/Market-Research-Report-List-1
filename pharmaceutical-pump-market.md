<p><p>The pharmaceutical pump market consists of various types of pumps used in the pharmaceutical industry. Centrifugal pumps are commonly used for high-volume and low-pressure applications, while positive displacement pumps are ideal for handling sensitive or viscous fluids. Jet pumps utilize the kinetic energy of a high-speed jet of liquid to create suction and move fluids. Air lift pumps use compressed air to lift and transport fluids. Each type of pump serves specific purposes and is chosen based on the requirements of the pharmaceutical processes.</p></p>

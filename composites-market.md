<p><p>Composites market types refer to various manufacturing processes used to create composite materials. Layup involves layering individual fibers or fabrics with resin to form a composite. Filament winding uses automated machinery to wrap continuous fibers around a rotating mandrel, creating a cylindrical composite. Injection molding combines resin and fibers in a mold, which is then subjected to heat and pressure to form the desired shape. Pultrusion pulls continuous fibers through a resin bath and molds them into a specific shape. Other market types may include compression molding, resin transfer molding, and vacuum infusion, among others.</p></p>
